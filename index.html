Getting Started

  1. Enter your details and click "Start Planning Session".
  2. Use the sidebar or dashboard cards to select a section.
  3. Add insights in the panel that appears. Click "Save & Close".
  4. Repeat for other sections. Progress is shown at the top.
  5. Go to "View Summary" to see all entries.
  6. From the Summary, click "Edit" to modify sections.
  7. When finished, click "Finalize & Download" on the Summary page to save your entry to the central database and download a PDF copy.
